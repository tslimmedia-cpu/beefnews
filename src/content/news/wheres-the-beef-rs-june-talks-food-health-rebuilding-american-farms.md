**The fight for food sovereignty is personal.**

RS "Ruffshot" June grew up in Philadelphia—about as far from cattle country as you can get. His journey into the beef industry wasn’t planned. It was fueled by **loss**. After watching his mother struggle under the weight of a **broken food system**, he dedicated his life to **reconnecting families with real food, real ranchers, and real health**.

In his conversation with **Dr. Michelle Gamble on *Mommy Heal Thyself***, June breaks down the **urgent need to rebuild local food networks**, why **beef is medicine**, and how the **Beef Initiative is fighting to save American ranchers**—one handshake at a time.

### **Key Takeaways:**

🔹 **Food, Trauma & Innovation** – The modern food system is designed for profit, not health. June explains how **corporate control over food** has left families struggling with disease, disconnection, and dependency.

🔹 **The Beef Initiative’s Mission** – With **141,000 family farms lost in the past 5 years**, June lays out how **small-scale processing centers and direct-to-consumer beef** can change everything.

🔹 **Beef as Medicine** – From **Sean Baker’s 72-hour fast after eating regenerative beef** to groundbreaking research on **beef’s role in gut health**, June explores why **quality beef** is the missing key to human health.

🔹 **Bridging the Gap** – **City dwellers aren’t disconnected from their food by choice.** They’ve been **systematically separated** from local farmers. The Beef Initiative’s **BeefMaps.com** is changing that.

🔹 **A Radical Idea: Bus Trips to Ranches** – Instead of lobbying in D.C., why not organize trips to local farms? June and Dr. Gamble discuss how **visiting a ranch could be more impactful than any government petition**.

### **Closing Quote:**

*"We don’t need to fight the system. We need to build a better one."* — RS "Ruffshot" June
