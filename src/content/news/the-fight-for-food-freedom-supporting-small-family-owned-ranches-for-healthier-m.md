Hi. My name is Dixie and my husband Zeke and I have a small family-owned ranch in the rugged foothills of Wyoming’s continental divide. We sell grass-fed and grass-finished Beef, Bison and Lamb direct to consumer because we KNOW we have to be the change we want to see in the world.

Changing the food system must come from the ground up, literally. That is why we take every measure to ensure our soils are healthy, with carbon-farming methods and regenerative management; our high-elevation pastures nourish our animals year-round. With no feed-lots, no growth hormones, no antibiotics, no GMO, no grains, and no vaccines, we raise meat that is natural and nutrient-dense!
