**This Isn’t a Supply Chain. It’s a Global Cartel.**

The Big Four—**Tyson, JBS, Cargill, National Beef**—aren’t just dominant. They’re a syndicate.

Controlling **85% of fed cattle slaughter**, they dictate prices, suppress competition, and extract profits from every level of the food chain.

[![](https://pub-13be57bb7a6645c6b332813889aed01e.r2.dev/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/image-20.png)](https://data.ers.usda.gov/reports.aspx?ID=4045)

Meanwhile, [**ranchers keep less than 35% of the beef dollar**](https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/food-dollar-series/)—down from 60% in 1980. The U.S. cattle herd sits at a 70-year low. Consumers pay record prices. Ranchers lose money per head.

And Brazil’s **JBS**—backed by [**corruption charges**, **Amazon deforestation**, and **laundered cattle**](https://beefnews.org/delist-jbs-the-cartel-linked-meat-giant-hiding-in-plain-sight/)—just keeps growing. In 2025, they’re still flooding U.S. shelves under a *[bullsh\*t](https://beefnews.org/red-white-and-bullsht-how-the-wto-turned-product-of-usa-into-a-globalist-scam/)* **“Product of USA”** label.

###### **Futures Rigged, Retail Captured**

In 2019, [**R-CALF USA sued the packers**](https://www.r-calfusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/Cattle-complaint.pdf) for rigging futures markets and cutting slaughter capacity to suppress prices.

By 2025, [**JBS paid $83.5M to settle**](https://www.r-calfusa.com/r-calf-usa-urges-cattle-feeders-to-file-claims-in-83-5-million-settlement-with-jbs-in-cattle-antitrust-case/#:~:text=BILLINGS%2C%20Mont.%2C%20May%2021,the%20details%20of%20the%20settlement.).

In 2022, they paid [**another $52.5M**](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/jbs-reaches-icebreaker-settlement-beef-price-fixing-claims-2022-02-02/#:~:text=Show%20more%20companies,exploitation%22%20in%20the%20meat%20sector.).

[Tyson’s **SEC filings**](https://www.tysonfoods.com/news/news-releases/2020/6/tyson-foods-statement-department-justice-indictment-broiler-chicken#:~:text=SPRINGDALE%2C%20Ark.%2C%20June%2010,fines%2C%20jail%20time%20or%20prosecution.) admit ongoing antitrust exposure.

No one broke them up. They paid. They moved on.

The squeeze continues.

###### **WTO: Where Sovereignty Goes to Die**

The [**repeal of MCOOL**](https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/RS22955) in 2015 wasn’t market-driven. It was WTO-enforced.

The U.S. folded under foreign pressure—and now **Brazilian beef from [cartel-linked](https://beefnews.org/labeled-legal-raised-by-narcos-what-the-usda-wont-tell-you-about-imported-cattle/) suppliers gets labeled “USA”** in your grocery aisle.

**Wikileaks cables** confirm: U.S. trade officials pushed arbitration to favor multinationals as early [as 1973](https://wikileaks.org/plusd/cables/1973GENEVA01320_b.html). Sovereignty was sold off to save face in Geneva.

In 2025, with [**screwworm outbreaks 400 miles from Texas**](https://beefnews.org/ranchers-demand-dhs-takeover-after-usda-fails-to-stop-screwworm-at-the-border/), the border was reopened anyway.
