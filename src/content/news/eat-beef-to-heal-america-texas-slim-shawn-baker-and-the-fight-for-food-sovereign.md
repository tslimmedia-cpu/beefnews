The latest **[Speaker Salon](https://www.speaker.salon/)** discussion, hosted by **Alana Newman** of **[Roses.Foundation](https://roses.foundation/)**, brought together **Dr. Shawn Baker, Texas Slim, RS "Ruffshot" June, and RanchMama Shanen** to dissect the critical role of **beef in health, food sovereignty, and America's future.** The conversation wasn’t just about nutrition—it was about reclaiming local food systems, fighting against corporate control, and ensuring that the next generation has access to truly regenerative beef.

As Dr. Baker laid out the **science of meat-based healing**, Texas Slim and his team delivered a stark warning: **America's ranchers are under attack.** With **corporate consolidation, regulatory overreach, and foreign control of food production**, independent ranchers are being squeezed out, threatening the very foundation of our food supply. The panelists didn't just diagnose the problem—they laid out solutions, from direct-to-consumer beef to the fight for **Beef.com** as a digital hub for rancher-led food sovereignty.

### **Key Points Covered**

- **Beef as Medicine** – Dr. Shawn Baker detailed how beef-based diets are **reversing metabolic disorders, mental health conditions, and autoimmune diseases**, proving that real food is the real cure.
- **The Corporate Capture of Beef** – Texas Slim revealed how **four multinational corporations control 85% of America’s beef supply**, dictating prices and protocols while small producers struggle to survive.
- **The Threat to Local Ranchers** – RS June and RanchMama Shanen emphasized the loss of **141,000 family farms in the last five years**, making it clear that action is needed now.
- **The Beef Initiative’s Solution** – A **direct-to-rancher digital marketplace**, educational outreach, and peer-to-peer relationships **to rebuild trust in local food supply chains.**
- **The Fight for Beef.com** – A strategic push to **acquire Beef.com** and turn it into a **decentralized hub for ranchers, consumers, and the future of regenerative food.**
