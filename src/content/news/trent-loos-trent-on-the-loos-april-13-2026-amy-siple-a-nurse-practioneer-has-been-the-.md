FIGHTING FOR PATIENT-CENTERED HEALTHCARE: AMY SIPLE'S BOLD STAND On this powerful episode of Trent on the Loos, Trent sits down with nurse practitioner Amy Siple for an eye-opening conversation about her relentless fight against the growing control of the medical-industrial complex. Amy breaks down how today's healthcare system has drifted away from patients and toward profit-driven priorities, leaving everyday Americans behind. She shares her mission to restore compassionate, effective, and truly patient-centered care—putting people back at the heart of medicine where they belong. Amy also delivers a major update on her legislative victory, highlighting a significant step forward in challenging bureaucratic overreach and empowering healthcare professionals to do what's right for their patients. This is more than a conversation—it's a call to action for accountability, transparency, and real reform in American healthcare.
