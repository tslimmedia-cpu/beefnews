### FRIDAY, July 26, 2024:

#### Texas Taco Tango

4:00 PM - 6:00 PM (Collaboration Hour)

6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Kick off the feast with our Texas Taco Tango night. Experience the authentic taste of Texas with an all you can eat Texas Fajita and Taco bar. A wide selection of cuts from the Cattleman’s Feast Selections. Indulge in the rich culture and flavors that make Texas unique. More exciting details to come.

---

### Saturday, July 27, 2024:

#### Tomahawk Ribeye Eating Contest & International Cattleman's Feast

#### First Ever Tomahawk Ribeye Eating Contest

4:30 PM - 05:30 PM

Presented by Texas Slim, K&C Cattle, Meat Mafia, and Hometown Meat Market in association with the I Am Texas Slim Foundation.

Contestants will represent and sponsor a Rancher/Producer of their choice, consuming only the finest ribeyes and water in a thrilling one-hour contest.

#### International Cattleman's Feast

6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

A first-ever global celebration of beef brought to you by Texas Slim, K&C Cattle, Hometown Meat Market, and Johnny “Ol Butch.” Explore an all you can eat extravaganza of lost prime cuts of beef, enjoy a butcher workshop, and experience technical displays of the cleanest beef in the nation.

Join in a celebration of international flavors, from the Classic Cowboy to the exotic Gaucho and even Asian influences!
