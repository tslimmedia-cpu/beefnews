What if the future of health wasn’t dictated by pharmaceutical corporations but by ranchers, scientists, and local food systems? That’s the vision behind the **Sovereign Health Framework**—an alternative to the **centralized, top-down One Health Framework** backed by global institutions like the WHO and Bill Gates. This approach, championed by the **I Am Texas Slim Foundation** and **The Beef Initiative**, prioritizes food as medicine, independent research, and direct relationships between ranchers and consumers.

In this powerful discussion, **Texas Slim**, **RS "Ruffshot" June**, **Breeauna Sagdal**, and **Dr. Kat Lindley** break down the corporate stranglehold on agriculture, the rise of lab-grown meat, and the policy battles ahead. They reveal how decentralized, peer-reviewed research and direct-to-consumer beef sales can restore food sovereignty and empower communities to take back control of their health.

### **Key Points Covered:**

- **The Sovereign Health Framework vs. The One Health Framework**
  - One Health consolidates food, medicine, and research under monopolized funding.
  - Sovereign Health decentralizes research, promotes regenerative agriculture, and restores food integrity.
- **The Decline of Family Farms & The Rise of Corporate Agriculture**
  - U.S. farms dropped from 5.4 million in 1950 to 1.89 million in 2023.
  - 70% of the U.S. food supply is ultra-processed, fueling metabolic disease.
- **Big Ag, Big Pharma, and the Captured Regulatory System**
  - The top four beef packers control 85% of U.S. cattle.
  - Pharmaceutical and agribusiness corporations spend $33 billion annually to influence policy and public perception.
- **Decentralizing Research & Ranching with the Miller-Hazen Model**
  - Community-funded research prioritizing rancher-driven microbiome studies.
  - Transforming ranches into living laboratories.
- **The Future of Food Policy & The Fight Against mRNA in Livestock**
  - Breeauna Sagdal’s legislative advocacy in South Dakota and Texas.
  - Montana’s HB418 to ban mRNA vaccines in animals.
  - USDA labeling deception: Why ‘Product of the USA’ often isn’t.
- **Building Parallel Economies Through Beef Maps & Bitcoin**
  - How Cole Bolton turned Bitcoin into a sustainable cattle operation.
  - [BeefMaps.com](http://BeefMaps.com) as a tool to reconnect consumers with ranchers.
  - The importance of shaking your rancher’s hand.
