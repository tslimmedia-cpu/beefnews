Trump’s [76% tariff](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/07/addressing-threats-to-the-us/) on Brazilian beef was billed as a **blow to foreign meat**. But for **JBS**—the **world’s biggest meatpacker**, based in Brazil—it’s just **vertical integration with a red, white, and blue ribbon.**

Let’s be blunt: The name changed. The supply chain didn’t. **The cartel won.**

###### Brazil Takes the Hit- But JBS Doesn't Bleed

![](https://beefnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image-136-1024x547.png)

Source: [White House](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/07/addressing-threats-to-the-us/)

Brazil [exported](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/meatpackers-rethink-beef-exports-us-after-trump-tariffs-industry-lobby-says-2025-07-15/) **181,000 metric tons of beef** to the U.S. in H1 2025, worth over **$1 billion**. The **tariff hits August 1**—raising total duties to **76%**. Brazil’s industry group ABIEC, which includes JBS, warned exports would become **“[inviable](https://www.reuters.com/world/americas/brazil-beef-packers-estimate-1-billion-losses-if-us-tariffs-apply-2025-07-29/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)”**, estimating **$1.3 billion in lost sales**.

But JBS stock? **[Up 0.4%](https://www.google.com/finance/quote/JBS:NYSE?sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwiBzfSBneaOAxVbQjABHaSAAHAQ3ecFegQIYRAb&window=5D) on the day of the announcement.** **Trading stable at $13.60 USD as of July 30.**

Why?

###### JBS Already Owns America's Meat Rack

![](https://beefnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image-143.png)

The Batista Brothers of JBS

While the press screamed “Brazil loses,” **JBS laughed** from its **250+ global plants**, including **dozens in the U.S.**, and majority ownership of **Pilgrim’s Pride**—the country’s second-largest poultry processor.

- **U.S. operations = [37.5 billion](https://www.reuters.com/business/retail-consumer/brazils-jbs-reports-profit-521-million-first-quarter-2025-05-13/) reais in Q1 2025**, up 36% YoY
- **$200M U.S. [plant expansion](https://jbsfoodsgroup.com/articles/jbs-usa-announces-200-million-in-u-s-beef-investments)** completed earlier this year
- **Pork and poultry** offset any beef slowdown

As JBS CEO Gilberto Tomazoni told reporters:

*“The global trade war has had an **insignificant** impact” thanks to “diversified production across Brazil, Australia, and the U.S.”*

Translation: **We own the game board. You just changed one tile.**

https://beefnews.org/delist-jbs-the-cartel-linked-meat-giant-hiding-in-plain-sight/

###### Cut Imports, Raise Prices, Profit in the U.S.

Here’s the grift:

1. **Imports drop** due to tariffs
2. **Supply tightens** (U.S. production down 2% this year due to drought and [border issues](https://www.usda.gov/about-usda/news/press-releases/2025/07/09/secretary-rollins-takes-decisive-action-and-shuts-down-us-southern-border-ports-livestock-trade-due))
3. **Prices [spike](https://www.reuters.com/markets/commodities/trump-tariff-brazilian-goods-could-jack-up-us-burger-price-2025-07-10/)** on retail shelves
4. **JBS U.S. division sells more beef at higher margins**

And who’s paying for it?

American families, buying inflated beef. American ranchers, still forced to fund [Beef Checkoff ads](https://beefnews.org/sell-a-cow-fund-the-enemy-welcome-to-the-beef-checkoff/). And a market still labeled “Product of USA,” even when owned by a **foreign multinational** whose home country just got “banned”.

###### The Fallacy of "Buy American"- When JBS *is* American Beef

![](https://beefnews.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/image-138-1024x683.png)

Let’s connect the dots:

- Tariff stops Brazilian beef
- But **JBS U.S. beef** is still labeled as domestic
- **MCOOL remains dead**, [thanks to WTO rulings](https://beefnews.org/red-white-and-bullsht-how-the-wto-turned-product-of-usa-into-a-globalist-scam/)
- And **JBS** still controls a massive share of the beef supply, regardless of origin

So when the press says “Brazil loses,” what they mean is: **“JBS Brazil pivots to JBS America.”**
