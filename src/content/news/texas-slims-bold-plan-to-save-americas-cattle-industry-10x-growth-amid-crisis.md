Fresh from the success of the Cattleman's Feast and Tomahawk Ribeye Contest, Texas Slim and The Beef Initiative are setting their sights on a bold goal: to 10x the population of cattle in an industry facing significant decline. In a recent conversation, Texas Slim discussed the current state of the cattle industry, market challenges, and innovative strategies to sustain and grow the industry.

#### Understanding Market Declines

Texas Slim began by discussing a recent report from [Graham Landing Cattle Co.](https://www.grahamfeedyard.com/), which highlighted sharp declines in feeder cattle prices. These drops are indicative of broader issues within the commercial cattle market, where cattle are traded like commodities.

"We’re witnessing a reverse trend," Slim explained. "Record high prices at auction houses are plummeting because inventories are depleted. Everyone sold out to capitalize on high profits, leading to a collapse-like scenario."

[![](https://pub-13be57bb7a6645c6b332813889aed01e.r2.dev/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/image-1-1024x682.png)](https://www.grahamfeedyard.com/)

#### Causes and Implications

The conversation delved into the reasons behind this trend. With inventories at historic lows due to mass liquidation of herds for profit, the industry is on the brink of a significant downturn.

"It’s time to rebuild our herds," Slim emphasized. "Record retail prices will be short-lived, and without financial tools to support producers, we risk losing more of them." This consolidation of the cattle industry has already seen countless producers exit, [selling their herds due to high prices](https://hpj.com/2024/03/01/what-to-expect-from-cattle-prices-herd-rebuilding-in-2024/).

### **The Beef Initiative Playbook: Herd Share Programs and Reinventing Cattle Value**

Looking ahead, the Beef Initiative plans to introduce herd share programs for 2024-2025, offering consumers a stake in cattle herds. "This provides a financial lifeline for producers and secures food supply lines for consumers," Slim highlighted.

The ultimate goal is to reinvent the value of cattle, shifting focus from mere profit to regenerative practices and community support. "We’re getting back to the source of the seed of feeding our communities," Slim said. "This is an exciting time, a perfect storm where we can truly make a difference: one rancher, one cow, one Modern-Day Cattleman at a time."

### **The Future Vision**

Texas Slim envisions a future where the cattle population grows through community and collaboration. By fostering new generations of ranchers and maintaining traditional practices, they aim to sustain the industry for years to come. Despite the current challenges, the Beef Initiative's proactive solutions provide hope for the future of the cattle industry. By embracing direct-to-consumer models, leveraging influential voices, and introducing innovative financial tools, Texas Slim is paving the way for a more sustainable and prosperous cattle industry.
