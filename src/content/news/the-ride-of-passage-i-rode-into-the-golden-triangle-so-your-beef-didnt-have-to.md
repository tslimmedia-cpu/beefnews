## **WHEN THE OFFICIALS ARE THE OPERATION**

This is the part that doesn't surprise me. Not one bit.

I didn't ride to the Golden Triangle because I thought it was a tourism stop. I went because the Beef Initiative is built on one foundational truth: the middleman is the problem. The opaque system is the problem. The unverifiable chain of custody — in beef, in money, in human movement — is where the rot lives.

Deputy Finance Minister Vorapak Tanyawong resigned in October 2025 after allegations surfaced that his wife received more than $3 million in crypto proceeds from the networks he was charged with dismantling. Provincial Electricity Authority officials — assistant governor level — were arrested for running illegal crypto mining operations inside the scam compounds, collecting monthly bribe payments of up to 400,000 baht to keep the power on and the inspectors away.

January 2026. Chiang Dao District — the exact stop on my 2023 route where I filmed a video about a failed cabbage crop. Six officials arrested. Two deputy district chiefs. Selling fake Thai residency documents, birth certificates, and nationality papers to Chinese nationals linked to northern scam networks.

The Anti-Corruption Organisation of Thailand called 2025 [the year of "Networked Corruption"](https://www.nationthailand.com/blogs/news/general/40060324) — their chairman naming it explicitly: not isolated bad actors, but patronage networks operating as a system, extracting rents from every flow that passes through weak-governance border zones.

The [2025 US Trafficking in Persons Report](https://www.state.gov/reports/2025-trafficking-in-persons-report/thailand) flagged it too — corrupt Thai officials protecting agriculture and poultry business owners from inspections, migrant labor trafficking risks running through the same source countries feeding the scam compounds, official complicity impeding enforcement at every level.

The [United Nations confirmed it in February 2026](https://www.ohchr.org/en/press-releases/2026/02/un-report-details-grave-abuses-against-those-trafficked-scam-centres) — grave abuses against trafficked persons inside the Golden Triangle scam centers, the same SEZ geography I documented from the riverbank.

I'm not a journalist. I'm a cattleman from the Llano Estacado who built a global #BeefIntelligence network because I understood something simple: you cannot fix what you cannot see. And the powers that be have spent decades making sure you cannot see.
